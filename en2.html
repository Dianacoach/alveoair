ABOUT ME

Hello, my name is Julio Bautista. I am a respiratory therapist with over 30 years of hands-on experience working in hospitals, rehabilitation centers,and nursing homes across the United States, including New York, New Jersey, Arizona, Hawaii, and Florida. Originally from Seville, Spain, Europe. I am fully bilingual in English and Spanish, and I provide support to individuals and families in both languages around the world. After decades in active clinical practice, I now focus on offering direct, practical guidance to people living with respiratory conditions, as well as to caregivers who often feel overwhelmed or unsure where to turn. This platform allows me to share real-world knowledge in a way that is accessible, clear, and focused on your day-to-day needs. My goal is simple: to provide reliable, easy-to-understand support when you need it most. Whether it’s learning how to use oxygen safely, choosing between concentrators or portable systems, improving comfort with CPAP or BiPAP therapy, or applying breathing techniques during daily activities, I’m here to guide you step by step. I also provide support with tracheostomy care, humidification, nebulizer use, and environmental factors such as air quality, allergens, and home setup. I understand that many companies and providers cannot always respond quickly, even when the situation feels urgent. Sometimes you may not need emergency services, but you do need timely help—within hours, not days. If you are dealing with equipment concerns, low oxygen supply, or uncertainty about what to do next, I can help you think clearly, guide you on who to contact, and assist in finding solutions as quickly as possible. This service is designed to give you more than just information—it is meant to help you feel supported, prepared, and safe, with someone experienced by your side when questions or concerns arise.
