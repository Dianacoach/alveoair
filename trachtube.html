Tracheostomy Tube Coaching

A tracheostomy tube (trach tube) is a small medical tube placed through an opening in the neck into the windpipe (trachea) to help a person breathe. It allows air to go directly into the lungs when normal breathing through the nose and mouth is difficult, compromised or blocked. It is commonly used for long-term breathing support, airway protection, or to assist with ventilators and secretion management.
