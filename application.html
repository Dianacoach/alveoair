Initial Review of Your History

This initial review is completed in written form to ensure accuracy and completeness. It allows me to provide faster and more precise guidance whenever you need support, without having to ask repeated questions each time.

By understanding your history, symptoms, and ongoing updates, I can respond more efficiently and help you take the right steps when concerns arise.

In some situations, I may guide you on what information to communicate to healthcare providers or emergency services so they can better understand your condition. However, this service does not replace emergency services or direct medical care.

To get started, please provide the following in writing by clicking below (Email or WhatsApp):

  • A brief cardio-respiratory history
  • Previous and most recent diagnoses
  • Equipment used in the past
  • Equipment currently in use (CPAP, BiPAP, masks such as full face or nasal, oxygen concentrators, oxygen tanks, nasal cannulas, tracheostomy tube—type and size if known)
  • Device details if available (name, model, and photos—especially if there are issues or damage)
  • Your current oxygen use (flow rate and when you use it)
  • Your activity level in daily living (ADLs)
  • Home environment factors (pets, carpets, air quality, location)

Providing this information carefully and completely will help me better understand your situation and support you more effectively.

Important:
This service provides guidance and education based on professional experience. It does not replace medical diagnosis, treatment, or emergency care. In case of a life-threatening emergency, please contact your local emergency services immediately.
